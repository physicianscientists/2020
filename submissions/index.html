Abstracts

Size Guidelines and Tips

  • Word Limit: 450 words
  • Restrictions: No tables, references, or figures
  • Tip: make the first sentence interesting
  • Tip: condense the methods but keep them thorough
  • Tip: keep conclusions accurate but generalizable
Submissions Closed

For more information, visit: http://jointmeeting.org/poster-session/

Posters

Size Guidelines and Tips

  • Poster Size: 4 ft. x 4 ft.
  • Tip: short, descriptive, and imaginative titles catch more viewers
  • Tip: diagrams, graphs, and images direct viewers attention

Local Chapter Travel Award Competition

APSA is providing travel awards to local chapters that best promote APSA and its goals of developing young physician scientists' careers

  • Award Amount: $500
  • Judging Criteria: Best promotion of APSA and development of physician scientists careers
